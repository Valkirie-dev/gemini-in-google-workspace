= Unit 2: Gemini in Gmail

Gemini in Gmail integrates advanced AI capabilities directly into email communication, enhancing productivity through *AI-powered email drafting, smart replies, and email summarisation*. This unit explores how Gemini leverages large language models and contextual understanding to transform everyday email tasks for IT professionals.

* **AI-Powered Email Drafting with "Help Me Write":** Generate and refine email drafts using Gemini’s large language models grounded in conversation history.
* **Contextual Smart Replies for Rapid Triage:** Employ machine learning to create context-aware reply suggestions to accelerate email responses.
* **Email Summarisation and Thread Management:** Condense long email threads into concise summaries highlighting key points and action items.
* **Advanced Prompting and Contextual Grounding:** Use the Persona-Task-Context-Format framework to craft effective AI prompts and avoid generic outputs.
* **Security and Administrative Controls:** Understand enterprise-grade compliance and governance for responsible Gemini use in Gmail.
